网易云音乐在Ubuntu22.04上报错闪退

2022-12-13

由于猪场百年不维护网易云音乐linux版，导致最新官方只支持18.04
在22.04上dpkg不会报错，打开时无反应，终端打开提示

/opt/netease/netease-cloud-music/netease-cloud-music: /opt/netease/netease-cloud-music/libs/libselinux.so.1: no version information available (required by /opt/netease/netease-cloud-music/libs/libgio-2.0.so.0)

/opt/netease/netease-cloud-music/netease-cloud-music: symbol lookup error: /opt/netease/netease-cloud-music/libs/libgio-2.0.so.0: undefined symbol: g_module_open_full`

在/opt/netease/netease-cloud-music/netease-cloud-music.bash中倒数第二行加入cd /lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/后即可解决
